Energies 3 Tree, 6 Metal and 9 Fire are 'Dynamic'. This is similar to the Cardinal Modality of the Zodiac.

Dynamic Energies, as the name suggests, tend to be proactive and action-oriented. They are not afraid to make the first move and tend to act as a kind of 'spark' or ignition to get things started.

Dynamic Energies tend to be quite communicative and verbally expressive. They also have more of an emotional and reactive quality to them, especially when either provoked in some way or when passionate or inspired.

Dynamic Energies are generally much more extroverted than the other types. They can be extremely verbally expressive and emotional at times.

People in this group tend to process information quickly. They are generally more intuitive and typically have high emotional intelligence. This is not to say that they don't have a logical side too. However, the fundamental driving force behind their thoughts and actions is strongly connected to their emotions and 'what feels right'.
