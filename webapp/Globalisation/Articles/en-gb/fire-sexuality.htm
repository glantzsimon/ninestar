9 Fires are typically passionate and warm in relationships

9 Fires are typically the most passionate of the 9 Energies and express themselves with great charisma and warmth in relationships. They wear their emotions on their sleeve and let thei partners know where they stand.

Fire Energies often have quite a flamboyant nature, but they are not necessarily flirting with you

The Fire element makes these Energies very attractive to others. However, their "flamboyant" nature can be misconstrued as flirting when it isn't necessarily.

9 Fires tend to be spontaneous but can also be impulsive

Fire Energies are spontaneous and even impulsive at times. They tend to do much better with partners who can keep up with them.

Appearances are important to Fire Energies

Appearances are important to Fire Energies and they may be put off by slovenly looks and habits. Fires may be particularly aware of potential partners' eyes and even their mannerisms.

9 Fires are naturally intuitive and sensitive to their partners

Fire Energies are naturally and deeply intuitive and are typically very receptive and sensitive to the needs of their partners. However, they can also be self-centred.

Fires need acknowledgment and appreciation from their partner

Fire needs fuel to burn and those born under this sign often need the acknowledgment of their partners. They typically do not take criticism well and can end up quite introverted and lacking in confidence if they are not appreciated or criticised too much.
