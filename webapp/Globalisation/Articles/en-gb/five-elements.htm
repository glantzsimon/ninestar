Each of the nine energies are associated with one of the five elements: water, wood, fire, earth and metal. Each of the elements is associated with a specific stage in the development from birth to death, or in the cycle of light and dark.

The Earth element is often considered a transitional point between the stages. For example, it describes the phase between seasons when the weather could easily revert to the previous season for a few days and then suddenly to the next in an unpredictable way. However, the Earth elemeht is also sometimes placed after Fire, during the late summer.
