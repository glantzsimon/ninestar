Energies 1 Water, 4 Tree and 7 Metal are 'flexible'. They are generally easy-going and have a gentle, 'go with the flow' kind of attitude to life. They are generally happy to adapt to those around them.

Flexible types are good at seeing things from different perspectives and tend to be understanding, compassionate and patient with others.

People in this group also have quite a rational, logical way of processing information. However, there is a more fluid and dynamic quality to their thinkin compared to the Static types.

Flexible types can also have a slightly unpredictable nature at times. They may get bored easily and like change.
