Energies 1 Water, 4 Tree and 7 Metal are 'flexible'. They are generally easy-going and have a gentle, 'go with the flow' kind of attitude to life. They are generally happy to adapt to those around them.

Flexible Energies also tend to be more on the introverted side.

Flexible Energies are good at seeing things from different perspectives and tend to be understanding, compassionate and patient with others.

People in this group also have quite a rational, logical way of processing information. However, there is a more fluid and open quality to their thinking compared to the Static and Dynamic Energies.

Flexible Energies can also have a slightly unpredictable nature at times. They may get bored easily and like change.
