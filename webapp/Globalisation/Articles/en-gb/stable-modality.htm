Stable Energies are steady and solid, generally process information more slowly. They are pragmatic and risk-averse. They like to plan ahead and dislike change instigated by others.

Energies {soil}, {coreearth} and {mountain} are 'stable'. As you can see, they are all of the Earth elements. Earth is by nature steady, solid and reliable.

Stable Energies like to plan ahead and tend to have longer-term goals, which they are good at sticking to. They are pragmatic and can be single-minded when they set their mind on something.

Stable Energies thend to process information slowly and steadily, weighing up the pros and cons. They accumulate knowledge gradually over time and tend to have good long-term memories. They tend to be rational thinkers and don't like to take too many risks. They are more resistant to change, especially when it's not instigated by them. You could say they are the most stubborn of the three groups.

Stable Energies can be both extroverted and introverted, with the Yang Energies being more extroverted. However, they are generally much less verbally and emotionally extressive than the Dynamid Energies.

Stable Energies are the solid foundations on which society is built. Without them, there would be chaos.
