3 Tree Energies often have a high sex drive

Representing the full force of spring, 3 Tree Energies often have the strongest libido of all 9 Energies. Indeed, sex is usually of great importance to them. However, 3 Tree men, owing to the fact that their Fundamental Energy is in line with their gender, typically express their high sex drive in relationship, but don't usually get into trouble with sex addiction.

3 Tree men are typically strong and virile and their sexual expression

Regardless of their sexuality, 3 Tree men are often strong and virile in their sexual expression. They like to make the first move - to chase, so to speak - and are typically more attracted to more passive, Yin energies, although it is not strictly the case.

3 Trees bring their great spontaneity and energy to their sexual relationships

3 Trees are typically very spontaneous and expressive in everything they do and their sexual relationships are no exception.

3 Trees are often precocious in their sexual development and have a natural exploratory nature

Being the expression of spring, or morning, 3 Trees are often precocious and eager to explore, which can often result in a strong desire to experiment with different methods of love-making. It can also lead to them discovering their sexuality at a relatively young age.
