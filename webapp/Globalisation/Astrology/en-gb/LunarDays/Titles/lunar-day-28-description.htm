Day 28 – Waning Crescent

The energy of this day is soft, pure, and luminous—ideal for spiritual reflection and quiet integration. As the end of the lunar cycle nears, the 28th day offers clarity and wisdom if the earlier parts of the cycle have been lived with awareness. It's a good time to review your intentions from the new moon and see what has come full circle.

Gentle practices like journaling, slow movement, or devotional rituals are well-supported. This is a subtle and sacred day, often carrying a profound sense of inner peace for those who are attuned.
