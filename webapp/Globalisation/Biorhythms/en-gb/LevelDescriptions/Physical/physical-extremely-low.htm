Your physical energy is currently at the lowest point in the cycle. Make sure to take plenty of breaks and not to take on anything physically challenging during this period. You may feel tired at this time, especially if you have been very busy during the last cycle. Be gentle with yourself and take it easy and get plenty of rest.

Now is a great time to catch up on sleep, to eat nourishing food, meditate, go for gentle Nature walks, listen to some beautiful music, get a massage, watch an inspiring movie, or whatever helps you to relax and unwind. Passive activities, like reading or watching a film, are highly beneficial during this part of the cycle.

If you are someone who is prone to exhaustion, it's especially important to take care of yourself during this period and to recharge your batteries. Try to avoid or limit exposure to known stressors and energy intensive activities.

There are a number of natural remedies that can help to nourish the body, especially tonic herbs such as liquorice, Rehmania, Schisandra, Fo ti, Ashwagandha, Maca, Rhodiola rosea, Ginseng and shilajit. It's also a great time to eat nutritious home cooked food high in vitamins and minerals.
