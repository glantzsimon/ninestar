Favorable Period for New Beginnings, Resolution, and Reconciliation

This is a harmonious time for fresh starts, resolving past matters, and rekindling connections. The mind element fosters unity by integrating both intellectual reasoning and intuitive insight (6), creating a well-rounded and complete perspective. This clarity allows for meaningful decisions and deeper understanding in personal and professional matters. To further strengthen this sense of unity, it is essential to cultivate awareness of your emotions (7), as they provide the final key to balance and wholeness. Whether welcoming new life, settling unresolved issues, or reuniting with others, this period supports healing, renewal, and emotional growth.
