Supportive Transformation - Parent Child Relationship

The Character Energies are in a Supportive Transformation, in which one Energy supports or nourishes the next. This typically creates a strong emotional connection and mutual understanding.

In romantic relationships, when the Character Energies in a Supportive Transformation, it typically improves the chances of chemistry and also relationship harmony. However, the Transformation of the Fundamental Energies plays a more significant role in the overal harmony of the relationship.
