This has the potential to be a very harmonious combination. However, it might be better suited to friendship than romance.
Strengths

Both people share the same Solar Energy "Water". Water is perhaps the most intuitive and empathetic of the 9 energies and so there is a potential for an extremely deep and intuitive connection between these two people.

Water Energy is naturally flexible, easy-going and generally non-confrontational, so this connection is likely to be quite harmonious with little conflict or misunderstandings.

Challenges

Water energies do their own space and can be quite intoverted and withdrawn at times. Therefore, there is a risk that, over time, this quality could create distance in the relationship.

Another potential drawback is that, when spending a lot of time together, there could be a tendency to exacerbate some of the more challenging aspects of Water energies, such as being chaotic and undisciplined or lacking structure and boundaries.

As both energies share the same Solar Energy, there are generally many more similarities between them, which can lead to decreased magnetism and potential for challenge and growth.
