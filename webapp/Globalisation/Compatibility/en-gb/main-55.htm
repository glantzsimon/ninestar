This is a generally harmonious combination with good potential for friendship. However, conflict may arise if both parties wish to take the lead.
Strengths

There is a potential for great trust to be formed in this relationship. Both parties are typically reliable and supportive and when that goes both ways, great friendships are often the result.

There is also a great potential for mutual understanding and compassion. This can be tremendously helpful to 5 Earths as they frequently experience ups and downs in life.

Challenges

5 Earth people typially like to be at the centre of everything they do and are very group-oriented. As both people are Core Earth Energies, on the one hand, they may be able to work well together and support one another; however, it can also go the other way and conflict could arise if both parties want to take the lead.

Core Earth Energies can be over-ambitious and unpractical at times, and if both parties share this tendency, there is a risk they could make things worse and really get carried away. However, there is also a very practical, down-to-Earth side of 5 Earth energies, so they could equally help each other to adopt a more pragmatic approach.

As both Energies share the same Fundamental Energy, there are generally many more similarities between them, which can lead to decreased magnetism and potential for challenge and growth.
