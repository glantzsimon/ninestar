Solar Energies – Both Yang

This combination often leads to a strong sense of mutual understanding, as both people operate with a similar level of energy and directness. However, it can also bring a slightly higher risk of conflict, as two Yang energies may occasionally clash or compete.

In some cases, a Yang/Yang dynamic may lack the sense of mystery or contrast needed to sustain long-term chemistry, and the initial spark can begin to fade. This tends to be less of an issue if the Lunar Energies form a Yin/Yang pairing, or if either the Solar or Lunar Energies are in a Challenging Transformation.
