Career and Professional Qualities
Gifts
  • Natural leaders and catalysts—able to unify people and steer direction.
  • Highly motivated, ambitious, and persistent when inspired by purpose.
  • Excellent at problem-solving, innovation, and long-term strategy.
  • Comfortable with authority—suited to politics, diplomacy, business, or military roles.
  • Can turn hobbies or interests into successful professional paths.
Challenges
  • Struggles with teamwork—may be overly dominant or controlling.
  • Career path may swing between extremes of success and instability.
  • Solutions can be impersonal or large-scale, ignoring individual nuance.
  • May become erratic, obsessive, or unmotivated when disconnected from purpose.
  • Complex relationship with money—generous yet mistrustful or controlling.
Supportive Strategies
  • Align career with meaningful goals to maintain motivation and balance.
  • Develop emotional intelligence and collaborative skills in team environments.
  • Ground visionary thinking with practical planning and open feedback.
  • Stay humble and flexible, especially when managing others or working under authority.
  • Build financial trust and structure to support long-term stability.
