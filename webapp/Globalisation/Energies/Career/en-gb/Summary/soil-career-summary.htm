Career and Professional Qualities

Gifts
  • Supportive, service-oriented, and emotionally grounded
  • Thrives in teamwork and community-focused environments
  • Highly reliable, diligent, and accurate with details
  • Creates harmony, structure, and emotional cohesion in the workplace
  • Excellent at organizing, managing routines, and providing continuity
  • Frugal and steady with finances; careful and non-speculative
Challenges
  • Not naturally suited to leadership or decision-making roles
  • May become overwhelmed by responsibility or interpersonal tension
  • Can procrastinate, become overly perfectionistic, or avoid conflict
  • Needs validation and acknowledgment to feel secure in their role
  • Struggles with change, delegation, and assertiveness
Best Suited For
  • Support roles in caregiving, education, healthcare, and hospitality
  • Team-oriented, service-based environments
  • Work involving organization, emotional support, or practical care
  • Environments with structure, appreciation, and low competition
