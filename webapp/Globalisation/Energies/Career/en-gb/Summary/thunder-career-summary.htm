Career and Professional Qualities

Gifts
  • Exceptional strategic vision and the ability to see the bigger picture.
  • Skilled in planning, brainstorming, mapping, and environmental scanning.
  • Strong creative instincts with potential in the arts, public speaking, and performance.
  • Innovative thinkers who excel in fields like computing, research, consulting, and business intelligence.
  • Natural ability to detect energy dynamics in the workplace and sense emerging trends.
Challenges
  • Struggle with hierarchical authority; prone to withdraw or react strongly under pressure.
  • May lose focus or become sidetracked when expected to carry out detailed plans.
  • Not naturally economical—can overspend or overextend due to generosity and enjoyment of life’s pleasures.
  • Financial or HR roles requiring strict adherence to rules may create undue stress.
Ideal Work Environment
  • Autonomous roles where creativity and strategy are valued over routine execution.
  • Opportunities for innovation, brainstorming, and flexible structure.
  • Freedom to operate independently without micromanagement.
  • Environments that welcome visionaries and allow space for inspired thinking.
