2 Earth children need a lot of consistency from their parents, teachers. Promises should be kept and a steady routine put in place. Their character can be damaged if their caregivers frequently break their promises or fail to observe the moral standards they set.

Soil children tend to have a very diplomatic nature, even at an early age. They may often adopt the role of peacemaker when siblings and friends fall out.

These children have a strong sense of justice and are the first to let you know if they have been dealt with unfairly.

Soil children, with their strong presence of Earth element, thrive in Nature. Every effort should be made to give them as much access to pleasant, natural settings as possible.
