Gifts
  • Joyful, expressive, and emotionally radiant—brings light and laughter to others.
  • Highly intuitive and emotionally attuned—sensitive to the moods of people and spaces.
  • Emotionally elastic—bounces back quickly from setbacks or conflict.
  • Capable of great empathy and compassion when grounded in presence.
Challenges
  • Prone to jealousy, agitation, and emotional overreaction.
  • Can be theatrical or dramatic when hurt—may withdraw or lash out.
  • Tendency to mourn lost love or missed opportunities long after they've passed.
  • Emotions may swing quickly between highs and lows, creating instability.
Supportive Strategies
  • Practice emotional grounding—use breath, stillness, or nature to return to center.
  • Channel intense feelings into creative or expressive outlets.
  • Take time to process disappointment without amplifying it through story or drama.
  • Surround yourself with calm, supportive people who help you regulate your fire.
