Gifts
  • Emotionally grounded, humble, and steady
  • Compassionate and deeply sympathetic
  • Creates a stabilizing presence in relationships
  • Resonant and accepting of others’ feelings
Challenges
  • May feel misunderstood or unappreciated
  • Tendency to withdraw or shut down emotionally
  • Prone to quiet jealousy, pride, or worry
  • May suffer silently rather than express emotional needs
