Emotional Landscape

Gift:
  • Emotionally deep and resilient
  • Courageous in facing internal and external unknowns
  • Willing to explore the hidden and mysterious aspects of life
  • Capable of holding complex emotional states with grace
  • Quiet strength, able to endure emotional intensity without avoidance
  • Feels life deeply and responds with compassion and insight
Challenge:
  • May experience abysmal fear or existential dread
  • Prone to deep insecurity, indecision, or emotional paralysis
  • Can feel isolated, emotionally withdrawn, or cut off from support
  • Courage may turn into recklessness or contra-phobic reactions
  • May mask fear with impulsive or bold actions
  • Vulnerable to emotional overwhelm if not grounded
