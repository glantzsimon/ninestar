Gifts
  • Deeply compassionate and emotionally insightful.
  • Tender, empathetic, and emotionally supportive of others.
  • Driven by a desire for personal and collective emotional growth.
  • Emotionally healing presence when grounded and centered.
Challenges
  • Prone to emotional overwhelm and instability when ungrounded.
  • May become rigid, reactive, or withdrawn under stress.
  • Struggles to regulate strong feelings without suppressing them.
Supportive Strategies
  • Practice emotional grounding through breath, stillness, and reflection.
  • Allow emotions to move through without judgment or suppression.
  • Create safe emotional boundaries and routines for self-regulation.
  • Use emotional depth as a source of strength, not a burden.
