Finances and Resource Management

  • Gift: Steady, modest, and resourceful with money; naturally frugal and careful with spending.
  • Strengths: Good at budgeting, saving, and managing household or team resources. Finds value in security and long-term stability.
  • Challenges: May overextend themselves financially due to generosity or desire to help others; can struggle to assert boundaries around money.
  • Risk: Can be overly self-sacrificing or hesitant to prioritize their own financial needs.
  • Advice: Remember that caring for one’s own material wellbeing allows for more sustainable support of others. Financial self-care is a form of service too.
