Finances and Resource Management

  • Neither extremely wealthy nor impoverished — typically gets by with quiet resourcefulness.
  • Not well-suited to high-risk or competitive financial environments; may be easily swindled.
  • Can be impressed or influenced by displays of power and wealth, especially from the opposite sex.
  • Cautious and measured when dealing with non-monetary resources like time, energy, and relationships.
  • Generally aware of personal limitations and prefers to conserve rather than gamble.
  • Financial decisions are often based on meaning, sustainability, and emotional alignment rather than profit alone.
