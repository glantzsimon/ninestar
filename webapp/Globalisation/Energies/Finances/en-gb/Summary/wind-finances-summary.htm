Finances and Resource Management

Gifts
  • Strong potential to generate profit and financial success.
  • Affluent when grounded and supported.
  • Good instincts for timing and opportunity.
Challenges
  • Inclined to take financial risks without proper assessment.
  • Can be a spendthrift—emotionally driven or impulsive with money.
  • May struggle with long-term planning or financial discipline.
Supportive Strategies
  • Create clear financial goals and spending boundaries.
  • Seek practical advice before major investments.
  • Develop awareness of emotional spending habits.
  • Balance generosity with mindful resource management.
