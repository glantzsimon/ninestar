Health and Wellness
Gifts
  • Exceptionally strong constitution—resilient and enduring under stress.
  • High physical vitality when balanced and active.
  • Drawn to practices that restore inner balance and emotional clarity.
Challenges
  • Tendency toward extremes—either overly active or deeply stagnant.
  • Prone to digestive issues, immune imbalance, or circulatory problems.
  • Susceptible to emotional burnout, depression, or addictive tendencies.
  • May struggle with worry, anxiety, or over-responsibility.
Supportive Strategies
  • Engage in regular physical activity—moderate to vigorous exercise is ideal.
  • Prioritize grounding practices like yoga, T’ai Chi, Qi Gong, or meditation.
  • Maintain a balanced diet and avoid stimulants, sugar, and emotional overeating.
  • Incorporate massage, lymphatic movement, and consistent rest into routines.
  • Practice emotional expression and seek support when overwhelmed.
