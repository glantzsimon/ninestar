Health and Wellness
Gifts
  • Strong circulatory vitality and radiant energy when balanced.
  • Responsive to plant-based diets and gentle, flowing exercise.
  • Emotionally attuned—sensitive to the body’s energetic rhythms.
Challenges
  • Prone to circulatory and glandular issues—especially under stress.
  • Overstimulation may cause anxiety, nervous tension, or digestive upset.
  • Can burn out quickly when overindulging or not taking adequate rest.
Supportive Strategies
  • Favor calming exercises like Tai Chi, yoga, or walking in nature.
  • Eat a light, mostly vegetarian diet—avoid hot spices, stimulants, and heavy foods.
  • Get regular rest and time away from sensory overload or emotional drama.
  • Practice emotional regulation and nervous system care through breathwork or meditation.
