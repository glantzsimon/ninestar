Health and Wellness
Gifts
  • Strong constitution with natural endurance and stamina.
  • Responds well to structured exercise and breath-based practices.
  • Capable of maintaining long-term wellness through discipline and routine.
Challenges
  • Prone to lung conditions, skin disorders, and respiratory issues.
  • Suppressed grief and emotional tension may impact breathing or immunity.
  • Overthinking, posture imbalances, or migraines may result from mental strain.
  • Risk of overexertion and chronic stress due to internalized pressure.
Supportive Strategies
  • Avoid smoking or environments that tax the lungs and skin.
  • Practice regular breathwork, yoga, and cardiovascular movement.
  • Express grief or emotional weight through journaling, talking, or creative outlets.
  • Use the autumn season for intentional reflection and letting go.
  • Balance strength with softness—rest, relax, and release perfectionism.
