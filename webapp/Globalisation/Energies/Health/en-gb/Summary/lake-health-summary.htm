Health and Wellness
Gifts
  • Responsive to healthy structure, breathwork, and routine.
  • Can maintain glowing skin, calm digestion, and emotional balance with consistent self-care.
  • Benefits greatly from fermented foods and fiber-rich diets.
  • Sensitive to energetic environments—thrives in calm, supportive spaces.
Challenges
  • Vulnerable to overstimulation, erratic breathing, and nervous tension.
  • Colon, lungs, and skin are sensitive areas—susceptible to imbalance.
  • Tendency toward overeating, eating too quickly, or eating late at night.
  • Without structure, may drift into laziness or overindulgence.
Supportive Strategies
  • Limit or eliminate mucus-forming foods, especially dairy.
  • Favor cooked fruit over raw; avoid lifeless or over-processed foods.
  • Stick to consistent mealtimes and avoid frequent or extreme diet changes.
  • Establish daily rhythms and light movement routines to maintain physical and emotional clarity.
