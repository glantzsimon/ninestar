Health
Gifts
  • Strong, grounded constitution with natural physical resilience.
  • Responds well to regular routines, nourishing foods, and mindful movement.
  • Highly responsive to massage, stretching, and lymphatic stimulation.
  • Can maintain long-term physical vitality when balanced.
Challenges
  • Prone to depression, hopelessness, and overthinking—especially under stress.
  • At risk of hypoglycemia or diabetes—pancreatic imbalances are common.
  • Tendency toward hypochondria or obsessive awareness of bodily symptoms.
  • Can become sedentary—risk of weight gain or stiffness (especially hips and neck).
Supportive Strategies
  • Avoid sugar, alcohol, and processed snacks—favor mineral-rich, fiber-heavy foods.
  • Incorporate regular movement: walking, yoga, stretching, loosening exercises.
  • Use natural light or full-spectrum bulbs to maintain emotional and hormonal balance.
  • Schedule periodic massage to release tension and keep circulation flowing.
  • Balance reflection with physical activity—get up, stretch, and reset often.
