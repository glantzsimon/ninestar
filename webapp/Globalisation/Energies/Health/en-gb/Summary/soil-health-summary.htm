Health and Wellbeing

Gifts
  • Grounded and steady when in good health
  • Strong nurturing instincts can motivate better self-care when aligned
  • Responds well to structured routines, natural therapies, and nourishment
  • Beneficial response to massage, calming movement (e.g., yoga), and meaningful rest
Challenges
  • May neglect self-care due to focus on others
  • Tendency toward emotional over-involvement and self-doubt
  • Prone to overeating or poor food choices when unbalanced
  • Susceptible to blood sugar imbalances, lymphatic stagnation, and digestive issues
  • Fatigue, depression, and lowered immunity under stress
Recommendations
  • Avoid sugar, refined carbs, caffeine, alcohol, and junk food
  • Engage in regular, gentle exercise that supports lymphatic flow (e.g., rebounding, inversion therapy)
  • Incorporate warm, nourishing meals and calming spiritual practices
  • Practice self-care with intention, ideally linked to their sense of purpose
