Health and Physical Wellbeing

  • The liver and gallbladder are the primary organs associated with the Tree element and require special care.
  • Avoid eating 2–3 hours before sleep and refrain from overeating to support nighttime liver regeneration.
  • Late nights and nocturnal lifestyles can disrupt the liver’s repair cycle—prioritize restful sleep.
  • Eat plenty of green leafy vegetables and citrus fruits (lemons, limes, grapefruits); apple cider vinegar is also beneficial.
  • Consider supplements like milk thistle, dandelion root, and calcium d-glucarate; coffee enemas may support detoxification.
  • Balance vigorous exercise with deep relaxation; yoga, stretching, and isometric exercises are especially helpful.
  • Thunder Energies are mentally active and sensitive—meditation, grounding practices, and stress-reduction techniques are essential.
  • Avoid fatty foods, refined flours, dairy, alcohol, and psychoactive substances; smoking is strongly discouraged.
  • Follow a diet rich in complex carbs, fruits, and vegetables; limit animal foods.
  • Protect yourself on windy days, particularly from easterly winds, to maintain energetic balance.
