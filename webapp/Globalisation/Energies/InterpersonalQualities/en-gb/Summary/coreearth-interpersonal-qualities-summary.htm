Interpersonal Qualities
Gifts
  • Natural influencer—often at the center of relationships and group dynamics.
  • Bold, frank, open, and impartial in communication.
  • Draws people in need of stability, leadership, or clear guidance.
  • Capable of steering situations with strength and fairness.
Challenges
  • Can come across as bossy, controlling, or domineering.
  • Prone to rejecting advice or criticism—especially under stress.
  • May act rashly or aggressively when feeling threatened or challenged.
  • Tendency to become emotionally distant or overly calculating when unbalanced.
Supportive Strategies
  • Balance strength with humility—stay open to feedback and other perspectives.
  • Practice patience and emotional attunement in challenging interactions.
  • Use influence to empower others, not to control outcomes.
  • Ground communication in fairness, compassion, and mutual respect.
