Interpersonal Qualities
Gifts
  • Charismatic, outgoing, and socially magnetic.
  • Warm, generous, and quick to forgive—uplifts others with ease.
  • Skilled in public relations, emotional expression, and hospitality.
  • Stylish, cheerful, and naturally inspiring—radiates confidence and presence.
Challenges
  • Can become dramatic, vain, or self-absorbed when unbalanced.
  • Struggles with deeper emotional intimacy or consistency in friendships.
  • May be overly reactive or easily offended—sensitive to slights or lack of admiration.
  • Tendency to perform or seek attention rather than show vulnerability.
Supportive Strategies
  • Ground social interactions in authenticity, not performance.
  • Seek meaningful friendships beyond surface charm or validation.
  • Practice self-awareness and remain open to feedback from others.
  • Use emotional expression to connect rather than to impress.
