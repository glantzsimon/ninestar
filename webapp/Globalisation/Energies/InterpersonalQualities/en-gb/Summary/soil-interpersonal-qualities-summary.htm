Interpersonal Qualities – 2 Soil

Gifts
  • Warm, nurturing, and emotionally generous
  • Creates safety and trust in relationships
  • Diplomatic, tactful, and quietly supportive
  • Friendly, sociable, and community-minded
  • Emotionally receptive and patient with others
Challenges
  • May become submissive, overly accommodating, or a doormat
  • Struggles to assert needs or speak up when boundaries are crossed
  • Over-dependence on others for affirmation
  • Can become irritable, cynical, or petty when unappreciated
  • Tendency to passively “wait and see” rather than act directly
