Interpersonal Qualities
Gifts
  • Kind, compassionate, and emotionally sensitive.
  • Gentle presence that inspires trust and confidence.
  • Charismatic, eloquent, and naturally sociable.
  • Excellent listener and supportive counsellor.
  • Flexible, well-mannered, and emotionally expressive in healthy ways.
Challenges
  • Can be overly trusting, gullible, or easily swayed by others.
  • Struggles with setting boundaries or asserting personal needs.
  • May become evasive, vague, or emotionally inconsistent.
  • Risk of clinging to relationships or manipulating subtly for reassurance.
Supportive Strategies
  • Practice honest self-expression and clear emotional boundaries.
  • Learn to say no with kindness and maintain inner stability.
  • Surround yourself with relationships that respect your sensitivity.
  • Ground your empathy in discernment to avoid emotional overwhelm.
