Relationships

Gifts
  • Confident, loyal, and emotionally steadfast in committed relationships.
  • Selective and principled—seeks meaningful connection over casual encounters.
  • Direct communicator—clear about needs, boundaries, and expectations.
  • Protective and reliable—values trust, honour, and long-term commitment.
  • Capable of deep devotion when emotionally secure and mature.
Challenges
  • Can become dominant, demanding, or emotionally rigid under stress.
  • Reserved nature may limit spontaneity or emotional expression.
  • Vulnerable to criticism—especially around sexual performance or emotional intimacy.
  • May struggle to adapt to more fluid or emotionally expressive partners.

Supportive Strategies

  • Balance strength with emotional openness and empathy.
  • Invite playfulness and spontaneity into intimate dynamics.
  • Encourage supportive communication and avoid overly moralistic framing.
  • Allow space for emotional vulnerability without fear of judgment.
