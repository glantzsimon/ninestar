3 Tree / Thunder Women and Relationships

Gifts
  • Romantic, passionate, affectionate, and chivalric in love
  • Highly expressive, erotic, and confident in sexual relationships
  • Spontaneous and full of energy in both life and love
  • Vibrant and loving when allowed to express her free-spirited nature
Challenges
  • Prone to becoming love-sick, infatuated quickly, and losing her heart
  • Struggles with balancing intense passions, leading to emotional fluctuations
  • Can suppress emotional and sexual needs, causing strain in relationships

Sexuality

  • Strong libido, with sex being of great importance
  • Likes to take the initiative, often attracted to more passive partners
  • Curious and exploratory, often precocious in her sexual development
