3 Tree / Thunder Men and Relationships

Gifts
  • Romantic, with an amorous disposition and a chivalric love
  • Highly passionate and erotic, bringing energy and excitement to relationships
  • Vibrant and sensitive when allowed to express their playful, free-spirited nature
  • Strong sexual drive, with a natural ability to chase and express their desires
Challenges
  • May become love-sick or infatuated quickly
  • Can struggle with maintaining balance due to their intense passions
  • Tendency to suppress sexual and emotional desires, leading to frustration
  • May struggle with jealousy or emotional volatility, especially in matters of appearance

Sexuality

  • Strong libido, often the strongest of all Nine energies
  • Spontaneous and expressive in sexual relationships
  • Early sexual development and eager to experiment with different methods of love-making
  • Typically attracted to more passive, Yin energies, though not exclusively
