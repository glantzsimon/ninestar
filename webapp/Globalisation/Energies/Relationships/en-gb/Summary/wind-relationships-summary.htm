Relationships
Gifts
  • Romantic, expressive, and idealistic in love.
  • Emotionally mature, physically affectionate, and deeply magnetic.
  • Loyal and committed—seeks soulful, meaningful partnerships.
  • Draws others in with warmth, sensitivity, and alluring presence.
Challenges
  • May project fantasies onto partners and overlook reality.
  • Can be overly impulsive, trusting, or drawn to status and power.
  • Vulnerable to disillusionment, emotional inconsistency, or detachment.
  • Risk of affairs or emotional distractions when core needs go unmet.
Supportive Strategies
  • Take time to reflect before entering new relationships.
  • Seek clarity through open communication and emotional honesty.
  • Ask for trusted advice when uncertain or emotionally clouded.
  • Ground romantic idealism in real connection and shared values.
