Both Core and Character Energies are Yin and therefore in line with gender

A confident and integrated expression of femininity and sexuality

Women with this configuration tend to be confident in their gender identity and at ease with their sexual expression. There is rarely any internal conflict regarding self-expression, and they generally relate well to both men and women. Their inner and outer energies are aligned, giving rise to a strong sense of self and clarity in communication.

Strong ideals and expectations may delay the formation of lasting partnerships

These women often idealise romantic relationships and may carry a vivid inner picture of how a partner or relationship should be. Because of this, they may struggle to empathise with {PotentialMatesText} who don’t meet their expectations or emotional tone. They may not quickly notice when relationships begin to break down, often staying in unsatisfying or dysfunctional relationships longer than is healthy. When not in a relationship, they may remain aloof or detached, waiting for the ideal partner rather than compromising.

Comfortable with celibacy and unconventional expressions of sexuality

Despite having a strong and healthy sex drive, women with this configuration are not generally preoccupied with sexual fulfilment. They are able to enjoy periods of celibacy with ease and may even take a more spiritual or playful view of sexuality. Because they do not feel threatened by their sexuality, they are more able to explore intimacy on their own terms.

Ease with others, but possible blind spots with the opposite sex

These women are often admired by others for their poise and self-assurance. They are likely to have many friendships with {PotentialMatesText}, and they make excellent confidants. However, their lack of inner connection to the opposite gender energy may make it harder for them to truly understand {PotentialMatesText} on a deep emotional level.
