Spiritual Life
Gifts
  • Strong intuitive clarity—able to discern truth from illusion with ease.
  • Spiritual insights often come as obvious, lived realities rather than abstract theories.
  • Excellent at expressing spiritual truth through communication, art, or presence.
  • Uplifts others with radiant wisdom and heartfelt guidance.
Challenges
  • May be swept up in appearances, emotion, or drama without grounding.
  • Can confuse aesthetic beauty with spiritual depth if not rooted in clarity.
  • Needs simple, calm environments to access and share deeper insight.
  • May overlook the need for stillness, silence, or structure in spiritual practice.
