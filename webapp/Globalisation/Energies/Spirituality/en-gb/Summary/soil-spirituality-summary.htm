Spiritual Life

Gifts
  • Deeply devoted and service-oriented at heart
  • Spiritual strength through humility, steadiness, and compassion
  • Feels spiritually nourished through community, ritual, and shared purpose
  • Can be a powerful force of healing and support when self-doubt is overcome
Challenges
  • May confuse faith with blind obedience
  • Struggles with self-doubt, sadness, or quiet resentment
  • Prone to 'idiot compassion' — giving without discernment or boundaries
  • Can feel unappreciated or spiritually depleted if giving is not received
Supportive Practices
  • Self-empowerment meditations, chanting, devotional music
  • Spiritual community or group practice (sangha, ritual circle, etc.)
  • Practices that cultivate discernment and healthy emotional boundaries
