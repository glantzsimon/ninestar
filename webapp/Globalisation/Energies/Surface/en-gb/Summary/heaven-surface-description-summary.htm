6 Metal Social Expression — Precision, Sincerity, and Clear Command
  • Appears clear, principled, and organised—often seen as competent and mature.
  • Communicates in a direct, concise way—avoids emotional drama or vagueness.
  • May come across as blunt, overly formal, or intimidating if not softened with warmth.
  • Strong sense of personal ethics—often holds themselves and others to high standards.
  • Projects an air of precision and control—brings clarity and structure to group dynamics.
