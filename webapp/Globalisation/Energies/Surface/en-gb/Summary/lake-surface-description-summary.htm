7 Metal Social Expression — Charm, Lightness, and Thoughtful Ease
  • Appears light-hearted, charming, and relaxed—often witty and socially magnetic.
  • Has a calming and pleasant presence—others feel at ease around them.
  • Comes across as wise and thoughtful—people often open up easily in their presence.

  • Communicates with grace and humour—balancing levity with subtle insight.
