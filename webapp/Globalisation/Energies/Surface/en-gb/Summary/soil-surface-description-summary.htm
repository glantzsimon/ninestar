2 Soil Social Expression — Quiet Reliability, Gentle Diplomacy, and Practical Warmth
  • Gives off a steady, grounded, and reliable impression—calm and practical in conversation.
  • Comes across as friendly, supportive, and quietly helpful—often seen as trustworthy.
  • May appear reserved or overly cautious—takes time to warm up in new situations.
  • Often noticed for their diplomacy and tact—rarely speaks out of turn.
  • Can seem detail-oriented or even fussy in their attention to small things.
