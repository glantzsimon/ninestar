The Original Astrology of Nature

9 Star Ki is more than just a personality tool—it is a cosmic map of rhythm, resonance, and energetic flow. Based on the 4000-year-old Book of Changes (the I Ching), it forms the philosophical foundation of Feng Shui, Traditional Chinese Medicine, and macrobiotic living. This is astrology rooted in nature itself—following cycles of time that mirror the movement of the stars, the turning of seasons, and the unfolding of human life.

Uncannily Accurate, Deeply Compassionate

Despite our diverse upbringings, cultures, and personal stories, 9 Star Ki cuts through surface differences to reveal core energetic patterns that shape who we are, how we relate, and what we need to grow. Its insights are often strikingly accurate—highlighting both our strengths and the challenges that help us evolve. It reminds us that our gifts and vulnerabilities are two sides of the same coin, and that every energy has its own perfect role in the greater whole.

Cycles Within Cycles

The power of 9 Star Ki lies in its cyclical view of life. It maps the universal pulse of time—from daily fluctuations to monthly, yearly, generational, and even epochal influences. These spirals of change govern everything from individual mood to global affairs, helping us understand not just who we are, but where we are in the greater rhythm of life. Like tides or seasons, these energies move through everything—shaping people, communities, and entire eras of history.
