9 Star Ki Astrology - Ancient wisdom for the modern era

Greetings and thank you for visiting NineStar. Whether you are new to 9 Star Ki or even new to astrology as a whole, you've come to a great resource to find detailed and accurate information on this ancient and fascinating system of astrology.

9 Star Ki is possibly the oldest system of astrology in the world today. It is based off the 4000-year-old Chinese 'Book of Changes' (the 'I Ching'), whose principles are the foundations of Feng Shui, traditional Chinese medicine and acupuncture.
