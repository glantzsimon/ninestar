Lin (Approach)

Lin signifies progress, expansion, and the approach of positive change. It represents a time when new opportunities arise, and one's efforts begin to bear fruit. The future holds promise.

Success is close at hand, but this hexagram advises maintaining awareness and responsibility. Good fortune should not lead to complacency but should be met with careful planning and continued effort.

On a deeper level, Lin reminds one to welcome change with an open heart. Growth and transformation are natural aspects of life, and embracing them with gratitude ensures lasting success.
