Before Completion

Before Completion symbolizes transition, anticipation, and the final steps before achieving a goal. It represents a time when one must remain focused and patient.

This hexagram advises caution and perseverance. The final stretch of any journey is often the most challenging, requiring steady effort and precision.

On a deeper level, Before Completion teaches that true mastery is in the details. By finishing what has been started with care and intention, one ensures a smooth transition into new phases of life.
