Guan (Contemplation)

Guan symbolizes observation, reflection, and gaining a deeper understanding of the world. It represents a time to step back and assess the situation with wisdom rather than immediate action.

Careful contemplation leads to better decisions. This hexagram advises looking beyond surface appearances and seeking insight before proceeding. True leadership comes from seeing the whole picture.

On a spiritual level, Guan speaks of inner clarity. The more one cultivates awareness and mindfulness, the more aligned they become with higher wisdom. True vision comes from deep observation.
