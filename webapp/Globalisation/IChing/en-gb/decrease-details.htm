Decrease

Decrease represents reduction, simplification, and the wisdom of letting go. It signifies a time when shedding excess leads to clarity and balance.

This hexagram advises evaluating what is truly necessary. By eliminating distractions, unnecessary burdens, or material excess, one can focus on what is truly meaningful and essential.

On a deeper level, Decrease teaches that less can be more. By embracing simplicity and minimalism, one finds a greater sense of peace and purpose in life.
