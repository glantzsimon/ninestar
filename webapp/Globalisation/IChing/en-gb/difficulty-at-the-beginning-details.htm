Zhun (Difficulty at the Beginning)

Zhun symbolizes the struggles and challenges of new beginnings. It represents the chaos and uncertainty that often accompany the start of any endeavor. Just as a seed must break through the earth to grow, one must push through obstacles to achieve progress.

This hexagram advises perseverance and patience. The difficulties faced now are not signs of failure but part of the natural process of development. It suggests seeking support, staying flexible, and being willing to learn from mistakes to navigate the path ahead successfully.

On a deeper level, Zhun teaches that struggle is a necessary precursor to strength. By facing hardships with resilience and wisdom, one develops character and a greater understanding of life's rhythms. It is a call to embrace growth even when the way forward seems unclear.
