Duration

Duration symbolizes stability, endurance, and the power of consistency. It represents a time when perseverance and dedication lead to lasting success.

This hexagram advises maintaining commitments and staying true to one's principles. Short-term gains may be tempting, but true achievement comes from steady effort and integrity.

On a deeper level, Duration teaches that real strength lies in resilience. By aligning with natural rhythms and remaining steadfast in one's purpose, one builds a foundation that stands the test of time.
