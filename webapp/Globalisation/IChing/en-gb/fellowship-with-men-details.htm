Tong Ren (Fellowship With Men)

Tong Ren symbolizes unity, collaboration, and the power of collective efforts. It represents a time when shared goals and common values bring people together, leading to mutual benefit and progress.

Success comes through cooperation rather than competition. This hexagram advises seeking like-minded individuals and fostering strong relationships. Differences should be set aside in favor of a greater purpose.

On a deeper level, Tong Ren speaks to the interconnectedness of all beings. The more one cultivates openness and harmony with others, the more aligned they become with the natural flow of life. True strength lies in unity.
