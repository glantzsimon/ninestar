Gathering Together

Gathering Together symbolizes unity, collaboration, and the power of collective effort. It represents a time when bringing people together creates strength and shared success.

This hexagram advises fostering strong connections and aligning with like-minded individuals. Community and teamwork enhance progress and provide support in times of need.

On a deeper level, Gathering Together teaches that unity is a source of great power. By working harmoniously with others, one creates lasting impact and fulfillment.
