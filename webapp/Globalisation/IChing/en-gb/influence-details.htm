Influence

Influence represents attraction, harmony, and the subtle forces that shape relationships and events. It signifies a time when gentle persuasion and mutual respect bring about positive change.

This hexagram advises using patience and sensitivity in interactions. Forceful action may be counterproductive, whereas genuine influence comes from sincerity and understanding.

On a deeper level, Influence teaches that everything is connected. The energy one puts into the world comes back in various forms. By cultivating kindness and authenticity, one naturally attracts the right people and opportunities.
