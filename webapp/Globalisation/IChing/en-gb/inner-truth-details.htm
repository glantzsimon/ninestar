Inner Truth

Inner Truth represents sincerity, clarity, and the deep understanding that comes from within. It signifies a time when intuition and honesty guide the way forward.

This hexagram advises listening to one's inner voice and aligning actions with truth. External distractions should not sway one's convictions when the heart knows the right path.

On a deeper level, Inner Truth teaches that wisdom and authenticity create harmony. By staying true to oneself and acting with integrity, one fosters trust and alignment with life's flow.
