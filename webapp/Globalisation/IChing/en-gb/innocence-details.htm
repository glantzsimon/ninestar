Innocence

Innocence symbolizes purity, spontaneity, and trust in the natural order. It represents a state of being where one acts without ulterior motives, moving in alignment with truth. When actions stem from sincerity, success follows effortlessly.

This hexagram advises approaching life with openness and faith. Overthinking or manipulation will only disrupt the flow. Trusting in the rightness of things allows one to move forward with ease.

On a deeper level, Innocence teaches that wisdom comes from being in harmony with the universe. Letting go of fear and control allows for authentic expression. When one acts with a pure heart, the path unfolds naturally.
