Qian (Modesty)

Qian symbolizes humility and the power of quiet strength. It teaches that true success comes not from boasting or seeking attention but from maintaining a low profile and acting with sincerity.

In times of progress, modesty ensures continued success. Overconfidence or arrogance can lead to downfall, while humility allows one to adapt and grow. This hexagram advises listening more than speaking and acting with grace.

On a deeper level, Qian reminds one that humility is not weakness but wisdom. True power lies in self-awareness and the ability to serve others without ego. By cultivating modesty, one remains in harmony with life's flow.
