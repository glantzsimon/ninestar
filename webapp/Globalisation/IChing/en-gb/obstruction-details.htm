Obstruction

Obstruction represents difficulties, obstacles, and challenges that hinder progress. It signifies a time when external forces or internal doubts may create barriers that must be overcome with patience and strategy.

This hexagram advises persistence and adaptability. Instead of forcing one's way through resistance, it may be wiser to pause, reassess, and find alternative routes. Sometimes, obstacles present valuable lessons that lead to greater wisdom.

On a deeper level, Obstruction teaches that challenges are opportunities for growth. By cultivating resilience and learning from setbacks, one emerges stronger and more capable of navigating life's complexities.
