Opposition

Opposition represents divergence, contrast, and differing perspectives. It signifies a time when conflicts or misunderstandings may arise, requiring wisdom to navigate.

This hexagram advises seeking understanding rather than division. Differences can lead to growth when approached with respect and openness. Clashes should be handled with diplomacy rather than force.

On a deeper level, Opposition teaches that contrast is a part of nature. Just as day and night define each other, opposing forces create balance. By embracing diversity, one finds harmony within apparent discord.
