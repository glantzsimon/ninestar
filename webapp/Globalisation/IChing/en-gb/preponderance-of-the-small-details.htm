Preponderance of the Small

Preponderance of the Small symbolizes the power of minor efforts and attention to detail. It represents a time when small, careful actions lead to meaningful progress.

This hexagram advises humility and patience. Grand gestures may not be effective, but small, consistent steps bring steady results.

On a deeper level, Preponderance of the Small teaches that true strength lies in subtlety and precision. By refining small aspects of life, one creates a foundation for long-term success.
