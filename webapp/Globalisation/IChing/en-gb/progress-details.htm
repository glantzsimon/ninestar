Progress

Progress symbolizes advancement, success, and forward movement. It represents a time when efforts yield results, and one moves closer to their goals. The momentum is strong, and the path ahead is clear.

This hexagram advises taking advantage of favorable conditions. Now is the time to act decisively and make the most of opportunities. However, progress should be steady rather than reckless.

On a deeper level, Progress reminds one that true growth is continuous. Even in success, one must remain humble and mindful. By staying aligned with their purpose, one ensures lasting advancement.
