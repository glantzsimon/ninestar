Return

Return represents cycles, renewal, and coming back to one's true path. It signifies a turning point where one is called to realign with their essence and values. Just as the seasons change, life moves in cycles, and returning to the beginning brings new understanding.

This hexagram advises embracing new beginnings with openness. Even if one has strayed from their course, it is never too late to return. Small, consistent steps will lead back to alignment.

On a deeper level, Return speaks to the journey of self-discovery. Growth is not linear, and returning to one's core allows for deeper wisdom. By honoring life's cycles, one moves in harmony with the greater flow.
