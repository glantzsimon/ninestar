Splitting Apart

Splitting Apart signifies a time of disintegration and separation. It represents situations where forces are pulling apart, whether in relationships, organizations, or personal life. Such periods can be painful, but they also clear the way for renewal.

This hexagram advises recognizing when something is beyond repair. Clinging to what is breaking down may only cause further harm. Instead, one should accept the natural cycles of endings and beginnings.

On a deeper level, Splitting Apart teaches that destruction is often a precursor to transformation. Letting go of outdated structures allows for the emergence of new possibilities. By embracing change, one can move forward with clarity and strength.
