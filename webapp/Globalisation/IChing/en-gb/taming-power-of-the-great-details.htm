Taming Power of the Great

Great Power represents strength, influence, and the ability to shape circumstances. It signifies a time when energy is high, and opportunities for significant achievements are present. However, power must be handled with responsibility.

This hexagram advises using strength wisely. Recklessness or abuse of power can lead to downfall. True power lies in balance, self-awareness, and the ability to serve a greater good.

On a deeper level, Great Power teaches that strength is not just external but also internal. Mastery over oneself is the highest form of power. When aligned with wisdom, great energy leads to great accomplishments.
