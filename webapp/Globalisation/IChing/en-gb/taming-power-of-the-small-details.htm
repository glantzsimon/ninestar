Taming Power of the Small

This hexagram represents the power of small, gradual efforts. It suggests that great change is not always achieved through force but through consistent, subtle influence. Like wind shaping mountains over time, patience and persistence lead to success.

This hexagram teaches the value of restraint and careful planning. Rather than forcing outcomes, one should work steadily toward their goals, adjusting as needed. Flexibility and attention to detail will yield better results than aggressive action.

Spiritually, the Taming Power of the Small reminds one to trust in the slow unfolding of life. True strength lies in adaptability and perseverance. Even the smallest actions, when aligned with wisdom, have the power to create lasting change.
