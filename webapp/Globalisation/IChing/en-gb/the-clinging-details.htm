The Clinging

The Clinging symbolizes illumination, passion, and attachment. It represents the fire that brings warmth and light but also needs careful tending to prevent destruction.

This hexagram advises maintaining clarity and focus. While passion and intensity can be powerful forces, they must be directed wisely. Overattachment to ideas, people, or emotions can lead to imbalance.

On a deeper level, The Clinging teaches that true illumination comes from inner awareness. Just as fire depends on fuel, wisdom must be nurtured through continual learning and self-reflection. By mastering attachment, one finds true enlightenment.
