The Family

The Family symbolizes structure, responsibility, and the importance of relationships. It represents the foundation of society and the bonds that sustain it.

This hexagram advises maintaining harmony within relationships. A strong foundation at home leads to stability in all aspects of life. Respect, communication, and shared values are key.

On a deeper level, The Family teaches that true strength is built through unity and care. By nurturing meaningful connections, one creates a supportive and thriving environment.
