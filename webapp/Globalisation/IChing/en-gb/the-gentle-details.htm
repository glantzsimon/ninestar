The Gentle

The Gentle symbolizes flexibility, influence, and the power of subtlety. It represents a time when soft yet persistent action brings the desired outcome.

This hexagram advises patience and careful persuasion. Rather than forcing results, one should move like the wind—shaping circumstances gently but effectively.

On a deeper level, The Gentle teaches that strength is not always loud or forceful. By moving with grace and wisdom, one achieves long-term success.
