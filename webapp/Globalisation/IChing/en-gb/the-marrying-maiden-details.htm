The Marrying Maiden

The Marrying Maiden represents adapting to circumstances and finding one's place within existing structures. It signifies a time when compromises may be necessary for harmony.

This hexagram advises accepting situations with grace and flexibility. While personal desires are important, adjusting to reality brings greater stability.

On a deeper level, The Marrying Maiden teaches that balance is found in understanding one's role. By aligning with life's flow, one finds purpose and fulfillment.
