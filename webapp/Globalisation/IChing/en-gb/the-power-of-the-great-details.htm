The Power of the Great

Power of the Great represents immense strength and the ability to create significant change. It signifies a time when energy is high, and one's influence is at its peak.

This hexagram advises using power responsibly. Strength without wisdom can lead to recklessness, while disciplined action ensures lasting achievements.

On a deeper level, Power of the Great teaches that true power comes from within. Mastery over oneself leads to mastery over external situations. By wielding strength with wisdom, one makes a lasting impact.
