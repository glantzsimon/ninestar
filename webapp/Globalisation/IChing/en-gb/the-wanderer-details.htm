The Wanderer

The Wanderer represents movement, transition, and the search for new experiences. It signifies a time of exploration, whether physical, emotional, or spiritual.

This hexagram advises embracing change with an open heart. While stability is important, growth often comes from stepping into the unknown.

On a deeper level, The Wanderer teaches that home is found within. By cultivating inner strength and adaptability, one thrives no matter where they go.
