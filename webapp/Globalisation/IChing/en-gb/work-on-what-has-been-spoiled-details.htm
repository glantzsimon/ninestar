Gu (Work on What Has Been Spoiled)

Gu represents the need to address past mistakes and correct what has been damaged. It signifies a time for repair, cleansing, and improvement. Neglected issues must be acknowledged and resolved.

This hexagram advises taking responsibility and making amends where necessary. Avoiding problems only allows them to worsen. Healing and renewal come through honest effort and self-reflection.

On a deeper level, Gu teaches that nothing is beyond repair. Even the most difficult situations can be transformed with patience and sincerity. The path to wholeness requires courage and dedication.
