Twelve Month Outlook

The Twelve Month Outlook offers insight into the shifting energies that influence each month throughout the year. Just as the seasons bring changes in weather and activity, these numerological cycles guide different areas of focus, action, and personal growth. Each month is shaped by one of the nine karmic numbers, creating a dynamic flow of opportunities, challenges, and lessons. Unlike traditional calendar months, these cycles follow celestial rhythms rather than fixed dates, aligning with the movements of the stars to provide a deeper, more intuitive understanding of life's unfolding patterns.
