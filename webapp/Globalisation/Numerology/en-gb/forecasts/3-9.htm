A Period of Decisions, Creativity, and Caution in the Latter Half

This cycle begins with a surge of creativity and dynamic activity (3), making it a great time for artistic expression, innovation, and bold decision-making. However, while enthusiasm may be high, it is important to allow space for contemplation and reflection to avoid overextending or pushing too far (6). As the period progresses, challenges may arise, bringing a need for careful assessment and adjustment.
