The harvest is over. Winter is nigh.

This is a time we tend to retreat into our "cave". We often feel withdrawn, reserved and uncommunicative. It is a time for honest intropsection. However, it can also be an uncomfortable time.

It is possible to feel a bit bogged down and stuck during this year. We may also feel rather more rigid and stubborn; even pushy about certain things.

During this year, there may be a strong sense of the "calm before the storm", as during the following year we emerge into the spotlight of Fire Energy, which often leads to a change of direction. This can make us feel uneasy at times. However, sometimes there are radical changes within this year too.

We need to be loving, kind and patient with ourselves during this year.
