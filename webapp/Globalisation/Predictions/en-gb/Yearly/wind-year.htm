The seedling is growing rapidly with many leaves and shoots appearing.

This is a year of sustained growth. However, it is important to stick to the plans. Any change in direction during this time could result in "crops failing", so to speak.

During this year, we often feel optimistic, joyful and energetic. Life is often smooth and we feel "in the flow".

We may feel especially communicative during this year, there are plenty of opportunities coming our way and our relationships are flourishing.

However, it is important not to get carried away and to pace ourselves so as not to burn out.
