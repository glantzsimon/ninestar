Outwardly you may appear shy, cautious or indecisive at times. Conversely, at other times you may appear bubbly, sociable and outgoing. People close to you will find that you retreat into your cave from time to time, especially when upset or stressed. You can be single-minded in what you do, which can lead to obstinacy and fanaticism. You may also be liable to procrastination. You can be persuasive and tend to be trusting of others. Overall you have a steady, consistent nature with plenty of common sense.
