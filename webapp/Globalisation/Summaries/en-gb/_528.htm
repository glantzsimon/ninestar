Your chart is entirely made up of Earth element, which gives you great strength and consistency. On the surface you may appear quite quiet and withdrawn. You may need to retreat to your cave from time to time. However, you can deliver a powerful argument when provoked. Others naturally gravitate towards you for support, advice and inspiration. However, care must be taken not to overgive and exhaust yourself.
