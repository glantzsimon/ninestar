On the surface, you can appear gentle, quiet and unassuming. You tend to be trusting of others and are generally dependable and helpful. Your deeper nature, which is revealed to those close to you, or when under pressure, is more reserved. You have a quiet determination and even be proud and inflexible. You are quietly self-critical and do not take criticism from others well. You can be quite the perfectionist. Others naturally gravitate towards you for support, advice and inspiration. However, care must be taken not to overgive and exhaust yourself. You prefer to learn from your own mistakes and may feel threatened by advice given by others. Your fundamental nature is ambitious and solid. You have an enormous capacity to bounce back from difficulties. You may be materialistic.
