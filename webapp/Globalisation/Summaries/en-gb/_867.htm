On the surface you often appear easy-going, flexible and fun. You can be charming, and have the potential to make others feel at ease. You have quite a direct, bold character. You are very clear in what you believe in and are frank and to-the-point when you express your opinions. You can be a perfectionist and take great pride in what you do. You fiercely dislike criticism. You go about your work with great skill, prudence and common sense. You are not afraid of hard work. Your fundamental nature is strong, single-minded, reliable and determined. You prefer to learn from your own mistakes and may feel threatened by advice given by others.
