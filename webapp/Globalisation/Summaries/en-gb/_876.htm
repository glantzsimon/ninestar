On the surface, you appear straightforward, organised and logical. You are not afraid of expressing your opinion and are direct and frank. You have a natural style and elegance and may well appreciate the arts. Your principal driving force is your singleminded determination, which can best express itself concerning what is fair andjust in the world. Friends and family will find you flexible, easy-going, entertaining and witty. You prefer to learn from your own mistakes and may feel threatened by advice given by others. You are naturally charming and have the skill of making those around you feel at ease.
