Outwardly you may sometimes appear quiet or withdrawn. You can be cautious and sometimes prone to procrastination. However, your deeper nature is emotional, sensitive and trusting. You can be quite influential and persuasive and equally easily influenced by others. You may have a stubborn streak and can be impulsive at times. Your Fire nature can give you tremendous clarity, sensitivity and intuition can are capable of inspiring those around you. It is important for you to get recognition and appreciation from others, otherwise you can lose confidence. Care must be taken not to be overly vein or self-centred.
